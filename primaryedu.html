    Overview

    Little Folks School believes that every child is important. We strive to develop the true potential of each child.

    Our school community works together to create a stimulating hands-on place for learning that places the physical and emotional well-being of the child at the centre of a welcoming environment.

    Our Aim

    Every child matters. We are confident students will:

    • Settle quickly into our warm and caring schools
    • be inquistive learners in a place of joyful learning
    • be in an enviroment that inspires creativity and imagination
    • develop self-confidence and self-esteem

    A Wholesome Learning Experience

    Assessment

    Assessment is genrally tracked informally and used in a formative manner. Various tools are used to guide improvement for each student, such as:

    • Baseline
    • Student Learning
    • Portfolios
    • Student Observation
    • Phonics Assessments
    • Reading Progress Checks

    Parental Involvement

    Parental engagement is the basis of a strong home-school partnership. To facilitate this, we believe in the following:

    • Exchanging knowledge about a child's needs, activities, interests, and progress
    • Parent-Teacher Meeting (PTMs) and Individual Parent-Teacher Meetingd (IPTMs) Provide an opportunity to discuss active home-learning strategies and progress in school

    Blended Learning

    Blended learning integrates technology and digital media with traditional classroom activities allowing students to customise their learning experiences.

    • All our schools are Microsoft Schools and have Microsoft Teams features, such as real-time video, collaborative space and more.
    • Our E-nursery/computer labs are integrated with curriculum-aligned applications.
    • Interactive whiteboards provide hands-on learning.
